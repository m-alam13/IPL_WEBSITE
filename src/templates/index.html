Welcome to Cric Analyzer

Cric Analyser is your comprehensive platform for all things cricket. Whether you're a passionate fan, a fantasy league enthusiast, or a data-driven analyst, we've got you covered. Dive into the world of cricket with our rich database of player statistics, match analysis, and team performance insights. Stay updated with live scores, news highlights, and expert commentary. From in-depth player profiles to strategic match predictions, we provide the tools and information you need to elevate your cricket experience to the next level.

Provides IPL insights

We provide details of IPL 2022 containing player names for each team, Player Details, Stadium Details and Title Sponsors

Team Management

Hii I am Arijit Ghosal, I designed the Frontend part of this website,with HTML,CSS and JS along with my friends Sk Murshid Alam who designed the backend part along with connecting with the database to the frontend, Sk Nasir Hossain who designed the complete database , ran queries for data retrieval, joining and other operations and Shehenaz Islam who was in charge of the presentation part for the complete project.

Database

The database consists of IPL insights from 2022 - Players Details , Player names from Six strong Teams , Stadium Details where the matches were played and Title Sponsors for Each Team


Privacy and Security

We prioritize the privacy and security of our website and Data, the database is kept securely and any changes in the frontend would not affect it, we are retrieving data using SQl queries, converting it to json fie and sending it to the frontend for display
